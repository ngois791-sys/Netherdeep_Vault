Maaya Two-Snake

  • Role Tide-broker & fixer
  • Race Human
  • Location Jigow, the Greying Wildlands
  • Status Alive — somewhere in town

NPC Jigow

A weathered, easy-smiling woman who seems to know every boat, every debt, and every secret along the Jigow waterfront. She wears two snake-shaped clasps in her hair, which she will tell you came from two very different people, and never the same story twice.

What the Party Knows

  • She steered them toward the Emerald Grotto, knowing full well how dangerous it was.
  • She seemed less interested in what they'd find than in how it would treat them once they had it.
  • She paid them in advance, in old coin from no mint anyone in Jigow recognized.
