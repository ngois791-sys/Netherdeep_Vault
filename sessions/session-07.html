Sirius Seatreader:

Ody:

Fi meets a tiefling grad student who is supposed to be investigating missing researchers for a big library in Marckett(sp?) but has gone rogue researching the mythic relics. The missing people were supposed to investigate Betrayer’s Rise but haven’t been heard from since. The history of our continent is a source of interest, before the Calamity the temple was a seat of power for the betrayer gods, it’s a beautifully made building and seems to have a life of its own. The war made its way to the temple itself and armies fought to seal the evil in the heart of the temple. Song: Unnamed hero who achieved much power during the war, almost achieving godhood: Apothian. Mythic Resonance is our new pal’s special interest; great acts leave impressions on the land, rippling out from the location of the event. A few months ago people from the Kobolds Soul Library Group came to the city on the word of Tenarian(sp?) Hythenos to investigate but lost contact after saying they’re going to go to the temple. Our new pal came with a group, they went to the temple but upon hearing voices and screaming she nope’d out of there. Ody has been listening to their convo but a lot of it is going over his head (easy to do) as he drinks a beer.

Torden is going to try and barter to detail his axe. While helping at the crematorium he sees a roc body that is not melting correctly. It’s being avoided by the other people present but a new tiefling demands to look at it. Mentioned that roc are scavengers and there might be things in the belly making it different from other birds; Torden slices it open without hesitation and there’s two gold rings and a pair of golden goggles. The goggles have a bird design on them, they’re Eyes of the Eagle, a magical item. Lastly there’s a box made of onyx that the tiefling grabs immediately; upon its removal the body begins to burn correctly. It’s a puzzle box but Torden’s massive fingers cannot manage the buttons. Prolix(sp?) The Tiefling cannot open it either; he’s another archeologist from Allegiance of All Sight, university in Markett(sp?). He’s here to look for interesting items for another party? He’s looking for a person as well, he’s spying on them?? Tall pale elf woman with silver hair: Aloysia of the Consortium of the Vermillion Dream. Branches in both continents' main capitals, the two groups are at odds with each other. CoVD is cultish and based on the Vermillion Moon, symbol of ill fortune. There’s an actual cult for this moon. Aloysia has come on some mission but Prolix is unsure why. Torden has maybe made a friend and gotten a pretty bird design on his axe.

Sirius and Ody buy a bunch of potions and meet Aloysia. Sirius is smitten, Ody is judgemental.

“You throw a hot evil elf at me HANNAH!!” - Nico

She’s been trying to get into the temple so Sirius pimps out Ody’s breaking and entering skills. Willing to pay us to get into the temple and acquire an item. (Cave of Wonders flashbacks lol) She has a small black stone with red veins in a pendant that she is looking for more of.

At the tavern Fi and unnamed tiefling are still babbling at each other. Group reconvenes. Question The Tiefling interrupts the party’s razzing of Sirius and his lack of rizz to let them know that she has a task for them to do. Tenarion(sp?) Hythenos is in a creepy house, she’s intimidated to go in alone. Necklace might have a connection to one of the heroes who fought in the Calamity. Hythenos is an arcane specialist, and might have info on our artifact.

Irvan has been having dreams and been increasingly quiet and withdrawn. He’s retired to one of the rooms and looks really tired and sick. He lets the party know that the headaches and visions seem to be improving the longer he’s in town.

Head to the Scooby Doo house, this guy is one of the oldest people in the city. He’s a drow with an odd reputation that’s been getting worse over the last few years. He’s the temple expert though. Two story manor with stone walls, old and established. It’s weathered the years and conflict with obvious effect, looking old and decrepit. There seems to be no one in the immediate vicinity. Sirius knocks on the door, no response. Well, time to see if the door will open. Orc asks our business, he’s a delivery man who hasn’t seen Hythenos for days at this point; he sent guards to do a wellness check and never heard anything from them…

“If we don’t come out in two hours, burn the house down.” - Nico

Strange amount of missing persons but with the rise in demon activity that might not be creating the level of concern you’d think. Sirius is worried about the potential correlation between increasing demons and missing persons. Into the foyer, big staircase and 4 doors. Paintings on the walls, goblins, a bugbear, some drow. Irvan is transfixed by the most recent painting, the bugbear. He knows this guy’s backstory and on the back of the painting is the name Brogun(sp?) Hythenos. All of the paintings feature that last name and Fi recognizes the name as one of the noble dens in Ryzona(sp?), not a biological family but an association rather.

Door 1: Sitting room, dusty and ill-kept, but expensive. Torden gets attacked by a sentient rug but manages to grapple his way out. “Can it open doors…no, no, it can’t.”

Door 2: A kitchen, no food, it’s been abandoned for a long time. Dusty pots and pans. Ody gains some silverware.

Door 3: Dining-room, same level of disuse. All the furniture is purple wood, it’s all set like it was ready to be used and the head of the table has moldy food and books, like it had been used some time ago. Fi creates a being of light and blinds Torden.

Door 4: A study, Sirius pushes Torden in first. It’s more lived in, floor to ceiling bookshelves, an oil lamp on a desk covered in papers. Lack of dust so it’s been used. Sirius finds a book on the gates of the temple, they’re called the Umbra Gates; designs on the gates change over time, as do the paths underneath the temple; the paths of the temple and its doors change and no one enters the same way. Torden finds a book almost immediately that seems to be a research journal, its theme seems to be about a rift in the temple. He’s obsessed with sealing the rift and rambles on and on about the betrayer gods holding sway over the paths even to this day. They may hold power over the things inside the temple. Fi finds a more recent journal talking about the kobold researchers asking about a lost shrine in the temple. Hythenos seems to have been excited about the idea that there might be a connection between the shrine and Avandra. Call back to, ‘praying to the changebringer in a place of evil’; it may well be the last bastion of good inside of the temple. Barwyn(sp?) is a name connected.

Hallway: Once we get rid of a spell, the hallway leads to stone doors, Torden throws them open like the doors of Helmsdeep. Windowless display room full of stone murals and other statues. Two black marble statues on either side of the door, pointing toward the collections. Looking at the stone carvings there’s a scene of abstract action; creature-like forms not really creating a scene but the bottom has been chiseled like it was hacked from something. The second one is more carefully gathered and shows a demonic entity towering over a bunch of humans. A statue of a horrific gargoyle. Please do not reanimate. Please. They all seem to have been removed from the temple. Last carving is smaller, a circle of nine distinct symbols:

  • Asmodeus - crown
  • Bane - flail
  • Groush(sp?) - bleeding eye
  • Loth(sp?) - spider
  • Zeheer(sp?) - snake
  • Tharasdune(sp?) - spiral
  • Vecna - Hand with eye
  • ?????
  • Tiamat - dragon’s claw

Display Cases:

  • Gold medallion - found on corpse on second level - aura of invocation
  • Rigid metal wand - found by an expedition 11 years ago - also invocation
  • Greatsword - missing plaque - has the bleeding eye on it - invocation/enchantment
  • Small stone box - says do not open - enchantment/abjuration
  • Small pouch of powder - sentimental rift related - weaker magical aura
  • Staff of wood - twisted by the magic of the gates - necromancy
  • Small cage, empty - ‘feed twice daily’ FML (loaf of bread size, chewed its way out)

The creature seems to have chewed through the ceiling of the storage closet, Ody is dragged away from the displays.

At the bottom of the stairs we find the body of an Aurora Guard; he was clawed but it wasn’t the cause of death. He might have died running for the stairs, he looks terrified which is not a great sign. At the top is more portraits and mirrors down a dark hall, five rooms total.

Door 1: Master bedroom, lived in, big bed, bookshelves, tall wardrobe. A journal and locked footlocker. Ody picks the lock and gets potions of healing and a scroll.

Door 2: Smaller bedroom, two knapsacks with symbols like Question’s notes. There’s notes and random items and it seems like they wanted to go into the temple even though they weren’t supposed to and they’ll just give it a go. Ody grabs the bags and decides to bring it all back to Question.

Door 3: Bathroom.

Door 4: Storage room, much more full. Sheet draped furniture and boxes. Inside a sheet covered wardrobe we hear scratching. Torden opens the door and there’s a large rat eating a cockroach. Fi tempts the ROUS out with jerky, long fangs, forked tongue, black leathery tail and double pupil. Demon ROUS is eating and Sirius is enamored. Ody is hiding on Torden while Fi lures it into one of her pockets.

Door 5: Fancy door, blood on the floor, it’s…off. There’s a magical ward on the door. Torden slams his axe into the wall next to the door and smashes through while Sirius and Fi are debating magical options. The second Aurora Guard is slumped against the door, bloody and dead. Large lamp-lit chamber, boarded windows, tables covered in notes, carvings and alchemic equipment. It’s clearly a research lab, bloody scratches on the walls, shattered benches and destroyed black stone urn in the center of the room. Hanging from the ceiling is an old drow man, head turned 180 and attacks. Horrifying.

Ody is terrified, panics and shoots. Misses and hides. The drow hits the ground and splits into two creatures. Fi uses heat metal to injure one of them. I’m not good at fight notes, lol. We fight. Once we beat his ass a shadowy jellyfish oozes out of the drow. Ody is less scared now but still hiding. Lol. It possesses the Aurora Guard. Dybik(sp?). Fi uses heat metal. After Sirius roasts the hell out of the corpse it comes out of the body again. Fi cannot use heat metal. Instead she tries to burn the house down because of course she does, makes a bonfire. More fighting. I like the jellyfish and now I’m sad. We find 3 vials of acid, sketches and notes on the items in the relic room. Sketches of rooms in the temple, pictures of a window with similar symbols to the one stone relic. Strange rubbings of an unknown language that makes us feel unwell. Sketch of cavernous room with bound figure, “Arms of Torog(sp?) open the way but beware his curse”. Drawing of a door with Avandra’s image, locked, no keyhole, cannot open as Barwyn(sp?). On a pillow and glass dome, black stone with red veins. Sirius wraps it up and puts it in one of Fi’s glass jars. It’s magic but unknown. (Maybe don’t give it to the scary hot elf lady Sirius)

And here we stop for now.
