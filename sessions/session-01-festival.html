Session 1

The Festival of Merit

In which strangers become a party, a contest is won, and the grotto gives up something it should have kept.

  • Played Year 0, Campaign Start
  • Location Jigow & the Emerald Grotto
  • In Game The week of the Festival

Session Jigow

The town of Jigow was loud with the Festival of Merit when our would-be heroes arrived — some for the contests, some for the coin, and at least one because a dream of cold black water would not let them sleep anywhere else.

What Happened

  • The party was thrown together in the festival's trials of strength and wit, and — more by luck than plan — came out on top.
  • A local broker, Maaya Two-Snake, took an interest in them and spoke of the Emerald Grotto on the coast, and of a prize worth more than any festival ribbon.
  • In the flooded tunnels of the grotto the party recovered a relic that hummed against the skin and made the water around it go strangely still.
  • As they surfaced, every one of them felt it at once: somewhere far below, in answer to the relic, something turned over in its sleep.

Loose Threads

  • Maaya knew the grotto was dangerous and sent them anyway. Why?
  • The relic is warm to one of the party and cold to the rest. No one can say why.
  • They were not the only crew asking about the grotto that week.
