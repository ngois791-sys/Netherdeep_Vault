History

Timeline

  • The Founding — Times Unknown

    • The Primordials shape the raw world. The gods arrive from beyond and remake it, and the first mortal races are born.
    • The Founding War rages between the gods and the Primordials, scarring the land before an uneasy order is won.

  • The Age of Arcanum

    • Mortal mages reach heights of power rivaling the gods themselves, raising floating cities and rewriting the laws of magic.
    • Alyxian is born a mortal hero, blessed by the gods and beloved of his people, a champion against the rising darkness.

  • The Calamity

    • The Betrayer Gods break free and war engulfs the world. Cities fall from the sky; nations are unmade in fire.
    • Alyxian's people die despite his prayers. In grief and pride he claims their faith for himself and ascends as the Apotheon.
    • The gods cast Alyxian down into the Lucidian Ocean, sealing him in the lightless trench that mortals would later name the Netherdeep.

  • The Divergence

    • The Calamity ends. The gods withdraw behind the Divine Gate, never again to walk the world directly.
    • The age of empires gives way to the slow rebuilding of the mortal nations.

  • ~30 years before

    • Trade between Marquet and Wildemount swells. Ank'Harel becomes a city of consortiums, scholars, and relic-hunters.

  • ~5 years before

    • Strange tides and stranger dreams begin to trouble the fishing town of Jigow on the Greying Wildlands.
    • Ruidus, the red moon, burns a little brighter in the night sky. The faithful and the fearful both take notice.
  • The present day

    Year 0 — Campaign Start

    • The Festival of Merit opens in Jigow, drawing crowds, contestants, and more than a few outsiders.
    • The party is thrown together by the festival's contests and a shared, sourceless dream of black water.
    • In the Emerald Grotto they find a relic that hums against the skin — and far below, something answers.
