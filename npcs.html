People

Important People

Aloysia

A mysterious elven woman. Tall, thin, pale, and dressed in crimson robes. Sirius and Ody first met her in a potion shop in Bazzoxan. She appears to be seeking out a strange black mineral with red veins running through it. She came to Bazzoxan seeking this mineral, and is hiring mercenaries to search for any trace of it. Prolix deeply distrusts her, and mentioned that she is a representative of a group called the Consortium of the Vermillion Dream, which the party doesn’t know much about yet.

Prolix Consortium of the Vermillion Dream Strange Mineral

Alyxian

A mysterious figure that you have only ever seen in visions. A young man with dark curly hair, appearing as a warrior wielding a shield and spear. All that you know of him so far is what you have seen and heard in visions. Nobody else around you seems to recognize his name. Ayo and her party have participated in a few of these shared visions and are aware of him too. Your party is currently in possession of an amulet that he seems to have once held: the Jewel of Three Prayers.

Ayo Jabe Visions Jewel of Three Prayers

Ayo Jabe

A young female water genasi, raised in Jigow. Leader of the adventuring group that the party competed against at the Festival of Merit. Has the respect of the others in her group, but is young, inexperienced, and hotheaded. Was present, along with the rest of her group, when the Jewel of Three Prayers was first discovered. Her and her team shared the first vision of the unknown warrior, Alyxian, along with the main party. Ayo held a grudge against the main party after learning that they had claimed the amulet while her party was unconscious. But she has since reached a tentative peace with the group (mainly with Torden), and agreed to mutually share any new information regarding the amulet/vision with the party.

Bairwyn

Elderly duergar woman. A cleric of the Prime Deity Avandra, and resident of a very old temple of Avandra in Bazzoxan. She has turned the temple into a lush vegetable garden that helps to supply the citizens of Bazzoxan.

Prime Deities Bazzoxan

◆ ◆ ◆

Dermot Wurder

A young male goblin, and a faithful cleric of the Luxon. He grew up in Jigow as a close childhood friend of Ayo, and is now a member of her adventuring party. Anxious and nervous, but kindhearted.

Ayo Jabe Jigow Luxon

◆ ◆ ◆

Elder Ushru

An elderly male goblin, one of the elders/leaders of Jigow. Has an affinity for puzzles, riddles, and mysteries. Helped the party to identify the golden amulet recovered from the Emerald Grotto as a possible Vestige of Divergence, called the Jewel of Three Prayers, and sent the group to Bazzoxan to seek more information.

Jigow Jewel of Three Prayers

◆ ◆ ◆

Galsariad Ardyth

A young male drow, and a skillful wizard. Seems to be the newest of Ayo’s group. Snarky, pretentious, interested in riddles and puzzles. As distrustful of the main party as they are of him.

Ayo Jabe

◆ ◆ ◆

Irvan Wastewalker

A young man and a local who grew up in Jigow. Child of roaming merchant parents who settled down in Jigow to raise him. Accompanied the party from Jigow to Bazzoxan after feeling called to Bazzoxan for reasons he doesn’t really understand. Has been experiencing strange dreams and memory-related confusion for the past year or so, and his mental condition has worsened since arriving in Bazzoxan.

Jigow Bazzoxan

◆ ◆ ◆

Maggie Keeneyes

A large, intimidating-looking female ogre with unusually bright blue eyes. Has a mercenary background, but has lived in Jigow long enough to have grown loyal to Ayo and her other party members. Has impressive wisdom and restraint, despite her tough appearance.

Ayo Jabe

◆ ◆ ◆

Prolix

A round, bookish tiefling first encountered by Torden in the crematorium in Bazzoxan, as Prolix was looking for interesting magical items. He is a representative of the Allegiance of Allsight, who was sent to Bazzoxan to keep a suspicious eye on Aloysia and try to figure out what she’s up to.

Allegiance of Allsight Aloysia

◆ ◆ ◆

Question

A small, extremely chatty young tiefling. A member of the Library of the Cobalt soul, in Bazzoxan to investigate two missing Cobalt Soul members that had gone missing from Bazzoxan a few months ago. Also in Bazzoxan (in a less official context) because of her enthusiastic interest in the history of the city and surrounding area. Question is actively developing a personal theory that she calls “mythic resonance”, which outlines her belief that acts of incredible, monumental courage or strength leave impressions on the very earth where they once occurred.

Library of the Cobalt Soul Bazzoxan

◆ ◆ ◆

Verin Theyless

A male drow member of the Aurora Watch, and part of a high-ranking noble family within the Kryn Dynasty. A loyal and dedicated commander of the Bazzoxan outpost. Visibly weighed down and wearied by his responsibilities: trying to contain the demonic incursions from within Betrayer’s Rise, protecting Bazzoxan, and keeping the Abyssal rifts a secret (by order of the Kryn Dynasty). Older brother of one of Phoebe’s childhood friends from Rosohna, Essek Theyless.

Bazzoxan Rosohna Kryn Dynasty
