Player Character

Vasha Tidemark

A water genasi paladin who hears a drowning voice in her dreams and means to answer it.

  • Class Paladin (Oath of the Deep)
  • Race Water Genasi
  • Player Sample
  • Status Alive & oath-bound

Player Character Paladin

Vasha grew up on a fishing skiff on the Lucidian coast, and she has always been a little too comfortable underwater — holding her breath past reason, hearing things in the current that the others on the boat swore weren't there. For most of her life she told herself it was nothing.

Then the dreams started: a man's voice, very far down, calling for help in a language she shouldn't know. She swore an oath to it before she even understood what she was promising. Now she follows that voice toward the Netherdeep, certain that whatever is down there is not a monster but a person — and that no one else seems to care whether it can still be saved.

Hooks & Ties

  • The relic from the Emerald Grotto is warm in her hands, and cold in everyone else's.
  • She is the only one of the party who admits to having the drowning dream — though she suspects she is not the only one who has it.
