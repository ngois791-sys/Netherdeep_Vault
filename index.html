The Netherdeep Vault

A living archive for our Call of the Netherdeep campaign — lore, session recaps, the people we've met, and the maps we've walked.

Long ago a mortal hero reached for godhood, and the world cast him down into the black water at the bottom of the sea. He is still down there, dreaming, and his dream is leaking into ours. This is Exandria in the age of the Apotheon's waking — of red Ruidus overhead, of relic-hunters and rival crews, and of a call that rises, faint and cold, from the Netherdeep.
