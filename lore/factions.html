Factions

Important Factions

The powers and groups shaping the party's journey across Xhorhas and beyond. Listed alphabetically.

Allegiance of Allsight

An archeological faction, seemingly in some kind of conflict or rivalry with the Consortium of the Vermillion dream. Prolix is a representative of this group, and was sent to Bazzoxan to keep an eye on Aloysia.

Consortium of the Vermillion Dream Prolix Aloysia

Consortium of the Vermillion Dream

Mentioned briefly by Prolix to be a competing/conflicting faction with the Allegiance of Allsight. Aloysia is allegedly a representative from the Consortium, and is in Bazzoxan to find any traces of a strange mineral on behalf of this group.

Prolix Allegiance of Allsight Aloysia Strange Mineral

Kryn Dynasty

The dominant power in Xhorhas, led by the powerful drow founder the Bright Queen Leylas Kryn. The capital city is Rosohna, which is where the Queen resides. The Aurora Watch constitutes the main military power on Xhorhas, maintaining law and order on behalf of the Queen.

The Bright Queen and her followers worship an unusual deity called the Luxon, and the Luxon has therefore become the major religion within Xhorhas. Worship of the “old gods” (Prime Deities) is not outlawed or discouraged within the Dynasty, but it is considered somewhat quaint, traditional, and old fashioned.

Rosohna Luxon

Library of the Cobalt Soul

A knowledge-seeking faction based out of the distant continent of Marquet, but with a presence in Rosohna. Question is a representative of this group, and was sent to Bazzoxan to seek out a few fellow members of the Cobalt Soul that had gone missing.

Rosohna Marquet Question Bazzoxan

The Luxon

The religion of the Kryn Dynasty, developed by the founder of the dynasty itself: the Bright Queen Leylas Kryn. The Luxon is a modern religion that does not involve any of the ancient gods/goddesses. Instead, worshippers revere the Luxon Beacons: large, mysterious, and powerful crystals in the shape of a dodecahedron that have been found in several locations around Xhorhas. This dodecahedron is the symbol for the faith, and exemplifies their tenets of light, rebirth, and possibility. Followed primarily by those that used to populate the Underdark (drow, goblins, orcs, etc.), but it has been spreading throughout the Kryn Dynasty for a few centuries. Verin and Dermot are both followers of the Luxon.
