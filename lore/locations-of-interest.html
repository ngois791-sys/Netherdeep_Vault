Locations of Interest

Notable places across Xhorhas and the lands of the Kryn Dynasty.

  • Category The World
  • Region Xhorhas

Jigow

A coastal town in northern Xhorhas, populated mostly by goblins and orcs. A number of drow, many of them politicians and soldiers of the Kryn Dynasty, reside here as well. Tension exists between the people who value the traditional ways of the village elders and those who prioritize the Kryn Dynasty’s more modern laws, culture, and religion (the Luxon). The town has a strong cultural tradition of fierce competition, and the local Festival of Merit was the setting of the beginning of the player’s journey.

Wastes of Xhorhas

Stretching across the lands of Eastern Wynandir is a valley that still bears the lingering scars of the final battles of the Calamity. Fields of broken earth and muddy paths weave around muggy swamps and corrupted forests, home to many demonic creatures twisted by the echoing shadows of the Betrayer Gods who once ruled this region. Fierce storms rule the sky as hungry predators stalk the open, desolate plains.

Xhorhas Calamity Betrayer Gods

Barbed Fields

The valley directly north of Rosohna and south of Bazzoxan is covered by a broken and marred landscape of jagged rock, sunken craters, massive spires of rock, and ancient, long-destroyed ruins. It is said that the final battles of the Calamity were fought on this valley floor. The earth is so clearly scarred and tainted here that few truly question the truth of this legend. However, powerful relics and armaments have been recovered from these ancient battlefields, and strange and unusual monsters roam the lands, so it is not uncommon for mercenaries to be sent into these fields by rich and/or curious benefactors and factions.

Bazzoxan

The military encampment of Bazzoxan keeps watch over the Betrayers’ Rise — a dark, evil temple that was instrumental to the Betrayer Gods during the events of the Calamity. Xhorhasian explorers and settlers built the foundation of a new city around the looming stone structures of this abandoned sanctum, until demonic forces from within the old temple threw the burgeoning society into chaos.

As discovered by the players during sessions #X and X, the city of Bazzoxan is now locked in a stalemate between the Kryn Dynasty’s forces (the Aurora Watch) and the demons that originate from an Abyssal rift within Betrayers’ Rise. This unholy site is the subject of constant research and growing worry for the dynasty, and also for the Commander of the city, Verin Theyless.

Rosohna *

The capital city of the Kryn Dynasty, and where Phoebe is from. Built on top of the ruins of a city with a dark past, Rosohna has since been reborn as a large, thriving city with tall spires and beautiful buildings, protected by the mighty Aurora Watch military and ruled by the original founder of the city herself: the Bright Queen Leylas Kryn. Rosohna is also the heart of the Luxon religion, and is populated mostly by races that once originated from the Underdark: drow, orcs, goblins, and duergar.
